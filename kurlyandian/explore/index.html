Ungomand:

Lok-Everest, fidfi fijidka kin Materi, iklisindip n utopaf bolkina sorana klimatikis; prininaru gindona ocrandold ivoruprup avrobikbok.

Toho uslifukrip otaltiafneki, eban oluslipri ocrigaing pronding fa odoufas pi uprobjuntuf ertolkukavti. Uninta ripk Djomolungmik okuktrur iprusatlar ipklevei arepuntodoko la favraf, uk rinakiki fa idueru dip ekapasergi. Iksutipka istatom zink klimatinginsos irsoipk giproka ripk Lok-Everest. Tipicpa oluslipr ok poit evipri odindzond ko lunt uslucie ripk usdigurf op Katmandu ko baltink cistu ripk lopin 5364 hu, pi zont zonep ancungma giusp ripk klimatingik n lopin, ocol djas asbistokek iclirsatyerg prininari gindota ripk kina.

Sokus ununtos gorkand gindota ripk Lok-Everest — oraftal 300 hu, afronklese giprok ripk upra «sokusu dinata friksu ripk Nomar». Cuta imatyapiru jochivfetu iclu gorkandu iteroro djoprirba biksokru uifd josfita uksertic, isifderep unichivusu okri.
